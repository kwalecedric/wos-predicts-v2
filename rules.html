❓ FAQs
When is the deadline to submit a pick? ▼
Picks must be submitted before the match kickoff time. Once the match starts, the predict button is locked and no changes can be made.
Can I change my pick after submitting? ▼
No. Once you lock in your pick it is final. Make sure you're confident before tapping "Lock in pick".
How does MOTM work? ▼
You pick a player you think will be named Man of the Match. If the official MOTM matches your player, you get 10 points. The admin confirms the result after the match.
What happens if a match is postponed? ▼
If a match is postponed or cancelled, all picks for that match are voided and no points are awarded. Your wildcard is returned if you used one.
How are points updated? ▼
Points are updated by the admin after each match result is confirmed. The leaderboard refreshes automatically once scoring is complete.
Can I be in multiple leagues? ▼
Yes. You can join multiple leagues using different league codes. Each league has its own separate leaderboard and prize pool.
What is the 5+ yellows pick? ▼
You're predicting that 5 or more yellow cards will be shown across both teams combined during the match. Worth 5 points if correct.
